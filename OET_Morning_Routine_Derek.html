12

6. Key Clinical Instructions
27
Finish all the tablets, even if you start to feel better before the course is done.
→ Antibiotics — completing the full course
✅
28
Take it every day, at the same time, even when you feel well.
→ Long-term medications — statins, lithium, warfarin
✅
29
Take it before you eat anything — on an empty stomach — with a full glass of water.
→ Alendronate / ferrous sulfate fasting instructions
✅
30
Stay upright — sitting or standing — for at least 30 minutes after taking it.
→ Alendronate — essential posture instruction
✅
31
Try to leave at least an hour between this tablet and your cup of tea, coffee, or dairy products.
→ Ferrous sulfate absorption — tea/coffee interference
✅
32
Take it with a meal or a snack to protect your stomach.
→ Metformin, antibiotics, ferrous sulfate if causing nausea
✅
33
The key is to keep your intake consistent — not too much, not too little.

✅
34
Please don't stop it without speaking to your GP first.

Always tell any doctor, dentist, or nurse that you take this medication before starting anything new.

If your skin or eyes turn yellow, see a doctor immediately — this can indicate a liver problem.

→ Metformin, antibiotics — reassuring about initial side effects
